Our Services

Accounts Services:

  • Yearly Accounts & Audit Report Preparation: We prepare accurate and comprehensive yearly accounts and audit reports to meet regulatory requirements and provide valuable financial insights.
  • TDS Compliance for Audit Report: We assist in complying with Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) regulations and ensure accurate reporting in the audit report.
  • VDS Compliance for Audit Report: Our team ensures compliance with Vat Deduct at Source (VDS) regulations and facilitates the correct deposition of VAT to the government exchequer.
  • Standard Accounts Maintenance: We maintain standard accounts in accordance with the Finance Act and the Income Tax Ordinance of 1984, ensuring adherence to legal and regulatory frameworks.

Income Tax Services:

  • Direct/Corporate Tax Services: Our team handles various aspects of direct taxation, including preparing withholding tax returns, salary payment statements, tax deduction from salaries, and employee return filing. We also compute quarterly advance income tax and assist in yearly tax return submissions for companies.
  • Other Tax Services: We provide tax health checkups, ensure compliance with bank transaction regulations, monitor VAT and tax compliance issues, and offer process improvement solutions to enhance tax compliance.

Indirect Tax (VAT) Services:

  • Major Activities VAT: We provide guidelines on VAT challan, purchase and sales register, and VAT price declaration, ensuring accurate and compliant VAT reporting.
  • VAT Return: Our team prepares and submits monthly online VAT returns, streamlining the VAT reporting process for businesses.
  • VDS (Vat Deduct at Source): We review transactions to ensure VAT deduction at source, facilitate timely deposition of VAT to the government exchequer, and issue Mushak 6.6 Challan for VAT deductions.
  • Other VAT Services: Our services include VAT health checkups, providing advice based on client requirements, liaising with VAT offices, calculating various VAT components, staying updated on VAT laws, participating in VAT authority hearings, and managing debit and credit notes.
